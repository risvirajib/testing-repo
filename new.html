Help Center

Get answers to your questions

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button at the top right corner of the page. Fill in your business information, verify your email address, and you'll be ready to start trading.

How can I find reliable suppliers?

We have a rigorous verification process for all suppliers. Look for the "Verified" badge on supplier profiles, check their transaction history and customer reviews. You can also use our Trade Assurance program for added protection.

What payment methods do you accept?

We support multiple payment methods including credit cards, bank transfers, and online payment platforms. For large transactions, we recommend using our secure Escrow service which holds payment until you confirm satisfactory delivery.

How does shipping work?

Shipping options vary by supplier and product. During checkout, you'll see available shipping methods and costs. We offer ocean freight, air freight, and express delivery options. All shipments include tracking information.

What is your return policy?

Our return policy protects buyers for quality issues. If you receive products that don't match the description or are defective, you can file a dispute within 15 days of delivery. We'll work with you and the supplier to resolve the issue.
